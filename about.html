About Tradewinds Automation & Engineering

Tradewinds Automation and Engineering was founded in 2023 with a clear purpose: to bring a practical, hands-on approach to improving manufacturing processes through thoughtful automation and modern technology.

The company was built on a deep appreciation for manufacturing environments— particularly within food and consumer packaged goods—where reliability, consistency, and efficiency directly impact the quality of products consumers trust every day.

Tradewinds was created to bridge the gap between cutting-edge automation capabilities and the realities of production-floor operations. This means designing solutions that work not just in theory, but day in and day out under real manufacturing conditions.

At the heart of Tradewinds is a passion for improving processes. Whether modernizing control systems, upgrading packaging lines, implementing CMMS platforms, or supporting capital projects, every engagement is approached with a focus on long-term value and sustainability.

“I founded Tradewinds to combine my passion for process improvement and automation with a commitment to helping manufacturers produce the highest-quality food possible. When systems run better, people work better — and consumers benefit.”

Our Approach

Tradewinds follows a structured yet flexible approach that ensures automation solutions are aligned with operational goals, technical requirements, and long-term reliability.

Understand

We begin by listening. Time is spent on the plant floor, understanding existing processes, constraints, and pain points from the perspective of operators, maintenance teams, and management.

Design

Solutions are thoughtfully designed to balance performance, usability, maintainability, and future scalability. The focus is on proven, well-supported technologies that fit the application.

Implement

Implementation is carried out with care and precision, whether through controls development, system integration, or on-site commissioning. Clear communication and coordination guide each phase.

Support

After startup, Tradewinds remains engaged to ensure systems perform as intended. Ongoing support, documentation, and continuous improvement help clients sustain success.
