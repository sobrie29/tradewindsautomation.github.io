Activities at Pacific Trails

Hiking

Pacific Trails offers guided hikes through redwood forests and along the rugged coastline. Trails range from easy strolls to moderate adventures.

Kayaking

Enjoy ocean kayaking with one of our experienced guides. Paddle through coves, observe marine wildlife, and take in breathtaking coastal views.

Bird Watching

The area surrounding Pacific Trails is home to a wide variety of bird species. Early mornings offer the best viewing opportunities.
