The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?

Our luxury yurts are permanent structures four feet off the ground. Each yurt is fully enclosed with canvas walls, a wooden floor, and a domed roof.

How are the yurts furnished?

Each yurt is furnished with a queen‑size bed with down quilt, gas fireplace, comfortable seating area, and electricity. A private bathhouse with hot showers is nearby.

What should I bring?

Bring comfortable walking shoes and a jacket for cool evenings. All bedding and towels are provided. A flashlight is helpful for evening strolls.
